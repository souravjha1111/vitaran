What Vitaran does ?

Vitaran is designed to take a step further in the development of smart cities by enabling efficient delivery system within cities to help locals and provide a source of income to them without being associated with a brand.It is a small step in creating smart India with efficient door to door delivery systems with well planned tracking system and authenticating people.

How Vitaran is different ?

Pool delivery

Vitaran offers pool delivery system of the products which also helps in creating clean environment.and promoting goodwill and harmony among the society.

Connecting local

Vitaran introduces a new platform where local logistic providers and consumers can interact and connect. The platform will provide an equal chance to rookie to compete with other heavy logistics companies. One-to-one interaction between consumers and dealers remove the concept of middleman in this sector, hence normalizing the prices.

Part time source of income

It will open doors to new job opportunities for both full time and part time employment facilities to people of any profession and promote self dependency among all citizens. Anyone can become the part of the delivery chain within 24 hours after proper authentication

FLEXIBLE PRICING

With Vitaran, local logistic providers and consumers can interact and connect, along with negotiating over the prices to enable easy door-to-door delivery of goods irrespective of their weight or size and their distance of transportation within the minimum price and time.The users can avail both fixed as well as bidding pricing systems .
